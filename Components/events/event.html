Quimica - Events & Workshops

Chem-It-Out

The Quimica society came up with its technical carnival, CHEM-IT-OUT at this year's TechMahotsav'23. We planned some exciting and nerve-wracking events for this tech-fest season under our department's banner. This TechMahotsav took the students on a voyage of zenith and served as a perfect launch pad for technology enthusiasts for showcasing their intelligence and inventiveness. It was, for sure, a massive platform for various activities, organized in the form of competitive events, guest lectures,seminars by notable personalities, technical workshops, and exhibitions. Quimica gave them the chance to pause, get fresh thoughts, and demonstrate their technical powers.

Chem-It-Out Events

Poster Presentation

The participants displayed their artistic talents in a fun event that included a model exhibition and poster presentation which was assessed by Dr. Amit Kumar Gupta (HOD of the Chemical department), Mr. Sunil Kumar Singh (professor in-charge of Quimica) Dr. Ashok Kumar Barnwal, and Dr. Ajay Oraon sir respectively.

Webinar

On the first day of the tech-mahostav'23, we hosted a thought-provoking webinar. It was an honor for us to take in sales wizard, Mr. Vinay Kariwala as he summarised business development on starting, growing, and scaling a successful company in these dynamic and quickly evolving industries.

Quiz Competition

An exciting and interesting quiz round was organized, including four rounds the rapid-fire round, the buzzer round, the individual Q&A, and the elimination. The rivalry was intense, and it came to a close when the Team Debuggers were declared the winner.

Seminar

An insightful seminar was organized in which, Mr. Rama Shankar, a distinguished reactor officer at the Nuclear Power Corporation of India Limited (NPCIL), BARC, Department of Atomic Energy, provided us with a thorough overview of the field of nuclear power, outlining its benefits, history, safety concerns, and potential in the future.

Conference

The seminar on carbon capture was delivered by Mr. Jaywardhan Kumar, a senior scientist at the CSIR (Council of Scientific & Industrial Research), in which he emphasized the technology's potential to lower greenhouse gas emissions, foster economic growth, and spur innovation in the energy industry.

Mesaventure's Round

The Mesadventure was an energizing event. Two distinct parts make up the event. The top 10 teams were chosen from an abstract submission round concerning an incident that occurred in online form during the first phase. The second round consists of a presentation of the incident and cross-questioning about a subject that has been discussed by the participants.

Other Events

Tech Kriti

A technical event with a two-tier brainstorming competition and exciting adventures. It's an event where participants are provided with an opportunity to show their creativity. It gave the students a chance to explore themselves and showcase their aptitude.

Shaastra

It was a three-tier event. An enthralling quiz followed by problem-solving rounds ended up with a mind-blowing competition among entrants. Industrial knowledge and interest of the participants were tested through this event among the four domains: Oil and Petroleum, Automobile, telecommunication, and Artificial intelligence.

Advaita

The captivating "ADVAITA- In search of wisdom" was an event organized on the eve of Independence Day which comprised of a welcome celebration for our guests and a three-tiered quiz competition with a strong emphasis on the application of science and technology which was an incredible and exciting experience for the students.

Scintilla

It was based on the idea of balancing the pros and cons of different products related to the chemical industry. The event was useful in developing the debating sense which is a key in many GD sessions.

Mesaventure

The two-rounds event, named MESAVENTURE's goal was to raise awareness about mishaps in various industries, i.e reducing the likelihood of mishaps in industries.The first round was of abstract presentation. In the second round i.e. presentation round, participants used PowerPoint to showcase their various models on the mishaps with critical analysis and comprehension.

Alumni Session

Dr. Santosh Kumar and Dr. Rajesh Ranjan, two of our most seasoned alumni, were a part of Quimica's most recent Alumni Interaction Session. They have discussed their personal experiences, their views on the need for further education, and some strategies for their success.

Seminar

MITACS-qualified seniors namely Miss Sakshi Gupta, Mr. Harsha Sinha, Miss Laxmi Sharma, Miss Mugdha Singh, and Miss Iysha Kumari answered queries regarding this Research Internship through the seminar. They answered all the questions about the Mitacs Globalink Research Internship.

Webinar

Quimica provided counseling for a multitude of competitive tests, including GATE, CAT, UPSC, etc. by bringing Mr. Ankur Bansal, the head of chemical engineering faculties at Unacademy Plus, and an IIT DELHI ALUMNI. He enlightened the students about various carrier options after attaining their bachelor's degree.

Workshop

A webinar with Miss. Riya Singh and Mr. Birendra Kumar, 2016 grads, on profile building and a plan for placements and internships were also organized. The webinar was helpful for the students and was designed to help them grow their profile and communication skillset and to expand their career skills.
